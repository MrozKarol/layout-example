THE PROBLEM

As much as we love our puppies every dog owner knows the pain of having to get out of a warm and comfy bed on a dark, wet and cold winter morning. Whether our outdoor clothes are safely stored away for taking up too much space, or just due to the usual morning rush, more often than not we are completely underdressed and cold.

THE IDEA

During another long and cold winter in Gda≈Ñsk, Poland in 2019, what initially started out with the "crazy" idea to create the ultimate dog walking suit for dog parents, inspired the quest to create a one of a kind piece of functional cold weather clothing, reimagining how we experience cold weather in our everyday lives.

RESEARCH

With a certain set of requirements and features in mind, we started our research by digging through our closets and browsing the internet. Spending countless hours in front of the computer screen and testing existing outdoor clothes, much to our surprise we were not able to find what we were looking for.

ANALYSIS

Our findings showed that most outdoor clothes are designed for specific performance outdoor activities, but not necessarily for everyday usability. Ski suits for example have a heavy protective exterior shell and usually require separate layers of insulation for optimal performance.

PROTOTYPING

Over time we developed a clear set of requirements and functionalities, so we set out to create such piece of clothing ourselves. While we did know what we wanted Walksey to do, without any actual experience in designing performance outdoor clothes, this process turned out to be more challenging than initially anticipated.

BREAKTHROUGH

After several failed attempts at trying to develop a working prototype, luck was ultimately on our side. As it turned out, Poland is world-renowned for its goose down as well as its down products. With some determination we were able to partner with fantastic local outdoor companies with over 30 years of experience in developing ultimate expedition outdoor gear.
